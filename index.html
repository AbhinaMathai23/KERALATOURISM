ABOUT

Kerala, a state on India's tropical Malabar Coast, has nearly 600km of Arabian Sea shoreline. It's known for its palm-lined beaches and backwaters, a network of canals. Inland are the Western Ghats, mountains whose slopes support tea, coffee and spice plantations as well as wildlife. National parks like Eravikulam and Periyar, plus Wayanad and other sanctuaries, are home to elephants, langur monkeys and tigers.Best Time to Visit Kerala Kerala has three tourist seasons. The month of September to March is considered as peak season of tourism in the state. Off season in Kerala which starts from May and Monsoon season from June to August. Each season in Kerala is unique making it an ideal destination for tourists who are visiting for various kinds of travel experiences in each of these seasons and Best Time to Visit Kerala differs on why you are visiting Kerala.
