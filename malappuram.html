Arimbra Hills

Arimbra Hills or Mini Ooty is one of the most scenic places to visit in Malappuram. The place is called mini Ooty because of its resemblance to the hill station of the same name, and a rather famous one at that.

Teak Museum

Teak-Museum is located 4 km from Nilambur, a town in the Malappuram district of Kerala, South India. Teak occurs naturally in India with the main teak forests found in Kerala.



Shanthi Theram park

Located on the banks of the river Kadalundi, the park has a number of activities to offer. You can be a part of the excitement of boating, that is available as both pedal boating and riding on a coracle (bowl boat), or as the locals call it, Kuttavanchi.
